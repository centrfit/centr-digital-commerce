# Head of Digital Commerce

**Reports to:** President, Digital
**Location:** Los Angeles (hybrid)
**Scope:** centr.com DTC, equipment + app bundles, paid acquisition, CRM, site rebuild
**Platform:** Shopify, Meta/Google Ads, CRO & experimentation tools, CRM, analytics stack

---

## About Centr

Centr is a performance fitness platform founded by Chris Hemsworth. We sell connected fitness equipment ($500–$3,000+), digital training subscriptions, and increasingly, bundles that combine both. We're the official equipment provider for HYROX — the fastest-growing fitness event series in the world.

Our direct-to-consumer business is early-stage and growing fast. We're not optimizing a mature machine — we're building a commerce engine from the ground up, with the brand, product catalog, and distribution tailwind to scale it significantly. This is a build, not an optimization.

---

## The Role

You will own the full digital commerce engine — from how customers find us, to what they see when they land, to what they buy, to how it gets to their door, to whether they stay.

That means paid acquisition, landing pages, on-site conversion, merchandising, bundling, CRM and early retention, demand planning, and fulfillment coordination. One person, one P&L, full accountability.

The strategic direction is set: build a connected commerce model where equipment and subscription reinforce each other. A customer buys a home gym and gets a connected training program. A subscriber sees equipment that makes their training better. Your job is to make that work — in the store, in the numbers, in the operations. You'll have strong opinions about how to get there. You'll also have a President who has strong opinions about where "there" is.

This is a hands-on role with a small team (3–5 direct reports across CRO, media, and CRM). You'll be in Shopify, in the ad accounts, in the data daily. You'll own the P&L. You'll fight for margin while pushing for growth. You'll coordinate across Brand Marketing, Warehouse, Product, Support, and Sales — and you'll need to be effective in a cross-functional environment where not everyone reports to you.

We're also rebuilding centr.com from the ground up this year (headless CMS, unified checkout, new brand experience). You'll be a key voice in that project — ensuring the rebuild serves commerce and conversion goals, not just design ones.

---

## What You'll Own

**Paid Acquisition & Media** — Run paid media across Meta, Google, and emerging channels. Plan, forecast, and manage spend to achieve efficient, scalable growth. Drive performance creative testing and partner with Brand Marketing on creative production. Connect demand creation with revenue outcomes.

**Conversion & Funnel Optimization** — Own the customer journey from ad click to purchase. Build and optimize landing pages for both subscription and DTC flows. Establish a real experimentation pipeline — hypotheses, tests, instrumentation, insights. Collaborate with Product and Engineering on onboarding improvements and early activation.

**centr.com Revenue & Merchandising** — Own on-site conversion, merchandising strategy, product prioritization, and promotional calendar. Make centr.com convert for $500–$3,000 products, not just accessories. Navigate MAP constraints and channel conflict with Retail and Sales.

**Bundle Strategy** — Own the equipment + subscription bundle end-to-end: pricing architecture, purchase flow, merchandising, attach rate. This is the single highest-leverage growth initiative in the business. Evolve it based on performance, operational constraints, and channel realities.

**CRM & Early Retention** — Shape the first 30–90 days of the customer journey: email, SMS, nudges, reactivation. Identify the behaviors that correlate with long-term retention and build campaigns that support them. Work cross-functionally with Content and Product on engagement drivers.

**Operations & Demand Planning** — Own revenue forecasting and coordinate with Warehouse and 3PL partners. You're accountable for the gap between what we sell and what we can actually ship. Balance growth ambition with operational reality and contribution guardrails.

**Site Rebuild** — Partner closely with Product and the agency on the centr.com rebuild. Ensure the new site architecture supports bundling, connected product storytelling, and conversion — not just brand aesthetics.

**Growth Analytics & Forecasting** — Own dashboards covering CAC, ROAS, conversion, AOV, retention, bundle attach, revenue, and efficiency. Build monthly and quarterly forecasts tied to spend, traffic, trials, subscriptions, and DTC orders. Make data-driven decisions quickly — knowing when to scale, pause, or shift.

- DTC revenue roughly doubled vs. prior year
- Equipment + app bundle established as a repeatable growth lever with measurable attach rate

- Higher early retention through more effective activation and CRM
